Dr. Revathi Selwyn

Dr. Revathi Selwyn lives in Hyderabad with her husband and has two sons. She loves nature, birds, and animals. Her love for nature and animals stems from her childhood days when she lived in a village surrounded by fields, forests, and other animals. The challenges and experiences that everything God created have a purpose inspired her to write books for children.
